Attendance Management

 

 

- Select on table Labor, information about Attendance table and Attendance information this labor will appear:

 

 

- Click on “TimeKeeping” button to timekeeping for this Labor:

 

- Choose Work date and Shift for this Labor, also information about is extra shift and is complete of this attendance then Click “Save” button:

 

       This attendance will be added in table attendance of this labor selected.

 

- Or “Cancel” button to cancel

 

- To edit an attendance you must select attendance from attendance list of the labor. Then click “Edit” button:

       + Change information about this attendance then click “Save” button.

 

- To delete an attendance you must select attendance from attendance list of the labor. Then click “Delete” button, confirm dialog will be appeared:

       + Click “Yes” button to delete, or “No” to cancel.
